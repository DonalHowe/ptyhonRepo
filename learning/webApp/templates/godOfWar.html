What is God Of War(2018) the game

God of War is the eighth installment in the franchise overall.
Unlike previous installments, this game focuses on Norse mythology
and follows an older and more seasoned Kratos and his new son Atreus in the years since God of War III.
The game released on April 20, 2018, and is exclusive to the PlayStation 4.
